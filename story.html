Our Story

Melanina Essentials ideology is to promote well-being. Our skin care products are all multi-use, made with high quality organic and natural ingredients. To further promote well-being, our products help support families of underrepresented communities in Brasil, with a sustainable source of income. In the development of creating Melanina, an opportunity to help improve the lives of others materialized, and the mission of Melanina was discovered. When you purchase Melanina, you help represent the underrepresented. It is our mission at Melanina to provide employment with a sustainable income to women of underrepresented communities, so that they may support their families, and improve the lives of themselves, their families, and their community.

Melanina Essentials was born in Brooklyn, NY, with the primary goal of creating a skin-care line with curative benefits. The ingredients for all of our products are carefully produced, and tested, with the goal of discovering a better way to heal the body, and improve over all health. Melanina is portuguese for melainin, the natural function of melanin in our bodies, is to protect and improve our immune system, all of the formulas created for Melaninna Essentials, is centered around finding ingredients that will improve skin and protect the immune system.

From Brooklyn, to Brasil, know that when you make the decision to use Melanina Essentials, you not only made a decision to improve your health, you also made a decision to improve the lives of others. Melanina Thanks You!
